Podcasts I Listen to While Driving

Abjura

Sometimes, all we need is a powerful story and meaningful wisdom to permanently change our lives and our way of thinking. This is the message of the award-winning podcast 'Abjura,' which received the New Media Award in 2017 for its impact and the depth of its topics. Bunya Al-Khamees aims to convey inspiring ideas to her audience through diverse episodes. She narrates readings, experiences, and rich information, connecting the past with the present and the East with the West. The podcast is accompanied by distinctive sound editing that stimulates the listeners' imagination.

Finjan

In this podcast, they delve into several different topics. There are no criteria or specific subjects, but one thing is certain: it offers a lot of fun and benefit.

Saturday's Couch

They delve into various social, psychological, and philosophical topics. It’s one of the best Podcasts to listen to on road trips.
